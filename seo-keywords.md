## Safe Positioning Notes

- Use "personal organization" and "educational budgeting guidance".
- Avoid saying the app gives regulated financial, investment, tax, legal, loan, or banking advice.
- Avoid promising that users will save money, pay debt faster, or improve credit scores.
- Use "helps you organize", "helps you compare", and "helps you understand" instead of guaranteed outcome claims.
