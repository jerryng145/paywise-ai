# PayWise AI Budget Planner FAQ

## What is PayWise AI?

PayWise AI is a personal finance app that helps users organize debts, repayment dates, payoff progress, and AI-guided repayment insights in one place.

## Is PayWise AI a budget planner?

Yes. PayWise AI works as a simple budget planner focused on debt tracking, repayment planning, payoff progress, and personal financial habits.

## Can PayWise AI track credit card debt?

Yes. PayWise AI can be used to track credit card balances, repayment dates, progress, and payoff planning.

## Can PayWise AI track PayLater or installment debt?

Yes. PayWise AI can help organize PayLater balances, installment payments, repayment dates, and overall payoff progress.

## Does PayWise AI support loans?

Yes. PayWise AI can help users track personal loans, car loans, home loans, and other debt repayment plans.

## What is the debt avalanche method?

The debt avalanche method is a payoff strategy that prioritizes debts with higher interest rates first while maintaining required payments on other debts.

## What is the debt snowball method?

The debt snowball method is a payoff strategy that prioritizes smaller balances first, helping users build momentum as each debt is paid down.

## Does PayWise AI give financial advice?

No. PayWise AI is for personal organization and educational budgeting guidance. It is not a bank, lender, investment adviser, tax adviser, legal adviser, or licensed financial adviser.

## Does PayWise AI require a login?

No. PayWise AI is designed to work without requiring a login.

## Is PayWise AI free?

PayWise AI has a free plan that lets users track up to 5 debts. Premium plans unlock unlimited debt tracking and deeper payoff planning features.

## What does PayWise AI Premium include?

PayWise AI Premium includes unlimited debt tracking, payoff strategy comparison, debt-free date insights, and debt reports.

## Does PayWise AI have a lifetime purchase option?

Yes. PayWise AI Lifetime Premium is available as a one-time purchase of $34.99.

## Is PayWise AI available on the App Store?

Yes. PayWise AI Budget Planner is available for iPhone and iPad on the App Store.
