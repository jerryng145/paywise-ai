What is a debt payoff planner without bank linking?

It is a tool for manually organizing debts, due dates, balances, payoff progress, and repayment plans without connecting a checking account, credit card account, loan provider, or transaction feed. This is useful when you want a clear debt picture but do not want a full bank-linked budgeting app.
